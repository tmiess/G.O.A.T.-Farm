Welcome to The GOAT Farm.

If you don't know by now, GOAT is an acronym for Greatest Of All Time. In modern meme times, GOAT is a moniker earned by athletes (or individuals in general) who have climbed to a level of craft and achievement in their field that can only be described as the best that ever was. Of course, like most topics in pop culture, the true GOAT is often a matter of great debate.

I would like to share my personal herd of GOATs, as well as discover who you believe are the true GOATs. You can view my GOATs, or you can add your own to my herd.
